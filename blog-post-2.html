Blog Post 2

Identify, compare, and critique 2 different deigns, July 13, 2022

Funko.com's Product Page

Overall: I feel that the overall design of this webpage is very basic and un-stimulating, however it is also still visually pleasing/clean/structured. I still think it’s an effective design in showing you available products, because they can clearly be seen.

Similarity: The size, shape, color, and behavior of each product image is the same. Similarly, the size, shape, color, behavior, and font of the action buttons are the same.

Continuation: The product images are aligned in rows.

Closure: The product images all together form one large rectangle.

Proximity: The product images are grouped closely together within a gray box.

Figure-Ground: The objects of focus are the header elements, all of the product images, and the sidebar text are the objects of focus.

Symmetry-Order: The shape that the product images make as a whole are symmetrical to the background box they’re within.
